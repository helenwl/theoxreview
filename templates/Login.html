theOXREVIEW

Students for students

Whilst theOXREVIEW has no official affiliation with Oxford University, it is a platform created for the undergraduate population. As a student, it's sometimes hard to know which optional modules to pick, or which options are seriously not recommended by past years, or to know who would be best to go to within college for help with an academic or pastoral problem. College mums and dads do a great job, but theOXREVIEW aims to be a much wider platform on which students can informally review tutors, lecture courses, and give helpful advice to others in their year/in the years below (something that has been done by word of mouth before!) In order to be a helpful community, we need users to also give advice/reviews as well, and encourage you to write about your experiences for the student community!
